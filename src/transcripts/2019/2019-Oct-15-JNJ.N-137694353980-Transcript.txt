Our strong portfolio of products and commercial capabilities has enabled us to deliver global growth at above-market levels despite significant biosimilar and generic headwinds.

DARZALEX continued its strong performance, growing about 57% globally or about 42% when adjusting for the impact of a favorable comparison to the prior year onetime reimbursement adjustment outside the U.S. The U.S. grew just over 26% and continues to benefit from strong market growth and about a 3.5-point increase in U.S. market share across all lines of therapy.
